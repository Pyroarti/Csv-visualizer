To use this application, follow these steps:

1. Click the "Browse files" button to select one or more CSV files to visualize.

2. Once you have selected your files, use the checkboxes to choose which components to visualize.

3. Click the "Show graph" button to display the visualization.

4. Use the date range picker and hour range slider to adjust the time period shown in the graph.

5. Draw a window to look closer at the values and double click to go back.
